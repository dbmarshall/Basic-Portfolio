About Me

I know my way around the front-end of web development, rising to peripheral challenges that have arisen over the years, a jack of all trades when needed. I've managed projects, done some SEO, and lots more. I've touched countless different technologies, lately focusing more on single-page applications.

When not doing any of that, I love to unplug in the mountains during the winters, an avid snowboarder who loves deep powder.
